About Todo & Expense Tracker

Todo Management

A structured task management system designed to help users plan, prioritize, and track daily activities efficiently. The Todo module focuses on simplicity, speed, and a distraction-free experience.

> Create & Organize:  Add and manage tasks seamlessly.

> Real-time Editing:  Update tasks instantly as plans evolve.

> Task Lifecycle Control:  Modify or remove tasks with full control.

> Local Data Persistence:  Tasks remain saved even after page refresh.

Expense Tracking

A budget monitoring system that enables users to record expenses, track total spending, and maintain financial awareness. Designed to promote disciplined spending through clear visual feedback and limit monitoring.

> Expense Recording:  Log expenses with accurate amount tracking.

> Automatic Calculations:  Real-time total expense computation.

> Budget Limit Setting:  Define maximum spending thresholds.

> Exceed Alerts:  Instant warnings when spending crosses limits.

Key Benefits

> Lightweight and fast performance.
