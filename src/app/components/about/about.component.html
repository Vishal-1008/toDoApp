This app is designed to help you stay organized and productive by allowing you to easily manage your to-do list. Whether you're tracking daily tasks, planning a project, or just keeping a grocery list, this app makes it easy to stay on top of things.

Key Features

> Add Tasks: Quickly add new tasks to your list.

> Edit Tasks: Made a typo or plans changed? Edit any task at any time.

> Delete Tasks: Remove tasks that are no longer needed.

> Save Tasks: All your tasks are saved to keep your progress intact.

How to Use

> To create a task: Use the input field and click "Add" to insert it into your list.

> To update a task: Click the edit icon next to any item, make changes, then save.

> To delete a task: Click the delete icon to remove it permanently.

> To save a task: All changes are stored locally, so your list remains even after refreshing the page.
